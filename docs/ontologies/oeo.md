## Ontology Overview

**Ontology Name:** Open Energy Ontology
**Abbreviation:** OEO
**Homepage:** https://github.com/OpenEnergyPlatform/ontology
**Primary Repository:** https://github.com/OpenEnergyPlatform/ontology
**Main Contact / Maintainer:** Mirjam Stappel (UOS) and Ludwig Hülk (RLI)

## Scope and Purpose

This domain ontology is a collaborative effort to represent the context of energy 
system analysis based on standard terminologies used by human experts in this field of 
research. 
It is designed to improve transparency and facilitate comparability and transferability 
of energy system modelling and scenario analysis. 
This ontology makes use of the Basic Formal Ontology (BFO) and its principles.
