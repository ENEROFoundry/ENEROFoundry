# Open Energy Ontologies Foundry

The OEO Foundry is an initiative for developing interoperable ontologies in the energy domain.<br>
It follows shared principles to ensure high-quality and FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable) ontologies.<br>
The goal is to develop a family of interoperable ontologies that are both logically well-formed and scientifically accurate.

## Mission

The mission is to establish a long-term organization that supports and guides both 
ontology developers and ontology users. <br>
It serves as a central point of contact for all interested stakeholders.
